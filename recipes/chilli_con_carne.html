Chilli Con Carne Recipe

Description

Chili con carne, meaning "chili with meat", is a spicy stew containing chili peppers, meat, tomatoes, and often pinto beans or kidney beans. Other seasonings may include garlic, onions, and cumin. The dish originated in northern Mexico or southern Texas.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice 75g
  • Kidney Beans 120g
  • Mince 70g
  • Chilli Powder 2tbsp
  • Salt 1tbsp
  • Chilli Sauce 100g
  • Chilli Con Carne Mix 20g
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Bring a pot to boil and add salt, then add the rice (stir regularly)
  2. Add oil to the pan and turn heat to medium
  3. Add the mince and kidney beans to the pan (to see if pan is hot enough drop a piece of mince)
  4. Add the chilli powder and salt, wait until mince turns brown
  5. Pour the chilli sauce and mix, stir until properly mixed
  6. Serve the rice and top with the cooked contents of the pan
  7. Add any toppings you fancy such as grated cheese or green toppings such as parsely
  8. Enjoy!
